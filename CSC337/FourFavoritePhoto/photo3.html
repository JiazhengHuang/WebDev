Great Pyramid of Giza

  • Country: Egypt
  • Fun Fact: This pyramid is the largest of all three pyramids that border El Giza, Egypt
    and it is also the largest pyramid ever built.
  • Description: The Great Pyramid of Giza (also known as the Pyramid of Khufu or the Pyramid
    of Cheops) is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex
    bordering present-day Giza in Greater Cairo, Egypt. It is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of
    the Ancient World, and the only one to remain largely intact.
