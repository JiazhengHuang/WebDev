The Great Wall of China

  • Country: China
  • Fun Fact: The wall is the longest man made structure in the world, with a total length of about
    13170.7 mi or 21196.18 km.
  • Description: The Great Wall of China is the collective name of a series of fortification systems
    generally built across the historical northern borders of China to protect and consolidate
    territories of Chinese states and empires against various nomadic groups of the steppe and
    their polities.
