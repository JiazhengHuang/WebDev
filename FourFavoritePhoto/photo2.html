Grand Canyon National Park

  • Country: United States
  • Fun Fact: The rock found on the upper rim (limestone) is around 230 million years old.
  • Description: The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in
    Arizona, United States. The Grand Canyon is 277 miles (446 km) long, up to 18 miles (29 km)
    wide and attains a depth of over a mile (6,093 feet or 1,857 meters).
